Frequently asked questions

Simple answers for small business owners creating staff training for the first time.

Do I need professional videos?

No. The Training Room is designed for simple phone videos. A manager can film a short workplace task, upload it and add written steps.

How long should a training video be?

Most training videos should be short and focused. Around 1 to 3 minutes is enough for a simple task, such as making a product, opening the store or completing a cleaning process.

Who creates the quiz questions?

Managers can write quiz questions based on the training video and written steps. The quiz helps check that staff understand the task before they mark it complete.

What should the quiz test?

The quiz should test the most important parts of the task, such as safety steps, correct order, ingredients, customer service expectations or common mistakes.

Can staff rewatch the training?

Yes. Staff can return to the training module whenever they need a refresher.

Can managers track who has completed training?

Yes. The manager dashboard shows who has completed the module, quiz scores and who still needs to finish.

Is this made for small businesses?

Yes. The Training Room is designed for small teams that need simple training without complicated enterprise software.
